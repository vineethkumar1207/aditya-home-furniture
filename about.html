Our Story

Aditya Home Furniture was founded on a simple premise: furniture should be built to outlast its owners. In a world of fast furniture and composite materials, we returned to the fundamentals of woodworking.

Working exclusively with solid European oak and plantation-grown teak, we create pieces that celebrate the natural imperfections and unique grain of the wood. Our designs are rooted in minimalism, allowing the material and the craftsmanship to speak for themselves.

Uncompromising Craftsmanship

Every joint is precision-cut, every surface hand-sanded. We utilize traditional joinery techniques like mortise and tenon to ensure structural integrity without relying on metal fasteners. This dedication to craft results in furniture that feels substantial and performs flawlessly generation after generation.

Sustainable Sourcing

We trace every piece of timber back to its source. Our European oak is harvested from FSC-certified forests in France and Germany, where selective logging practices promote forest regeneration. Our teak is sourced strictly from government-managed plantations in Southeast Asia, ensuring zero impact on old-growth rainforests.
