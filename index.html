Why European Oak & Teak?

We source only the finest sustainably harvested European oak and plantation teak. These materials are chosen for their extraordinary density, captivating grain patterns, and inherent resistance to wear.

Over time, oak develops a subtle, distinguished patina, while teak's natural oils provide unparalleled longevity, making pieces that can be passed down through generations.
